About this space

This 890 sq ft raw and stylish organic smoothie shop is located in Vancouver West Point Grey just a bus stop away from UBC. With its storefront directly on 10th Avenue, the shop is right at the center of a lively neighborhood surrounded by local corner stores from friendly bakeries to main banks and grocery chains.

When you enter the space, the community market shelves are facing the main shop area where walk-in clients enjoy their smoothies. This eye catching marketplace is perfect to look at gifty products and conversational pieces while waiting for an order to be prepared. The window facade overlooking the center of the shop is also great for hanging plants and greeneries while unique art pieces can be hanged on the main walls. The space filled with natural light is very inviting to discover and look at craft products up close.
