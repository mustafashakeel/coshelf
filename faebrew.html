About this space

Craft Coffee Bar in Richmond BC near Aberdeen Skytrain Station

Faebrew is an ultra-chic minimalistic craft coffee bar located in Richmond, BC, just a five-minutes walk away from Aberdeen Skytrain Station. It is a breeze to access from the main No. 3 Road with lots of traffic around. By car, there is plenty of parking spot right in front of the shop as well.

The coffee bar's interior boasts a contemporary gallery-like design aiming to attract a diverse range of sophisticated clientele searching for a handcrafted experience throughout. This space offers a high-quality stage for local artisans & artists to showcase their creations.
