Terms and Conditions

APPLICATION AND BOOKING

  • Maker agrees and honors all information provided to CoShelf to be honest and accurate.
  • Maker is responsible for submitting a completed application provided by CoShelf including a full product list, retail price and pictures intended for the rented space.
  • Maker application is carefully assessed and reviewed at the full discretion of CoShelf and the Retailer to ensure best fit in value alignment between all parties.
  • Upon approval, Maker shall receive confirmation from CoShelf along with a booking form to finalize payment of the selected rental space and duration.
  • The rental is finalized only upon payment received and confirmed.
UPSHELVING AND REMOVAL
  • Makers will receive a check-in and check-out schedule at time of booking and shall confirm upshelving schedule with CoShelf.
  • Maker shall deliver/retrieve to Retailer at the agreed upon check-in and check-out appointment to minimize significant disruption to Retailer’s day-to-day operation.
  • At each Maker’s appointment, a completed inventory stock list form with price list is required at the check-in to cross-check with CoShelf’s POS system.
  • Products with defects that might harm or endanger end consumers will not be accepted. If a product or any ingredients or materials used to make the product is recalled by a governmental regulatory body, CoShelf and the Retailer reserves the right to stop the sale of the said product, remove the product from the rented space and shall be returned to Maker.
  • Maker shall display products and marketing material at selected shelf space location, and shall remain there throughout booked rental period unless Maker formally files request and is approved for change by CoShelf.
  • Extra storage may be provided for over stock or packaging material if available.
  • Maker is responsible to ensure that the display and storage does not interfere with the business area such as the aisles and exits at the premise.
  • Maker shall comply with Retail location sales rules and procedures regarding display material during upshelving process. Makers are liable to the Retailer if any damage is caused.
  • Maker understands and agrees that CoShelf and the Retailer reserve the right to restrict the display of any item deemed unsuitable at their own discretion.
MAKER RIGHTS AND RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Maker retains full product ownership in the rented space until purchased.
  • Maker agrees to let the Retailer sell and handle payment transactions on Maker’s behalf.
  • Maker agrees to display only products pre-approved by the Retailer in the rented space.
  • Maker acknowledges that all approved products will be kept at the Retail Location for the rental duration until the event of a sale or rental termination, whichever occurs first
  • Maker agrees to display marketing materials in the rented space in a way that is respectful to the Retailer Location
  • Maker is responsible in ensuring the accuracy of the product list, retail price and pictures provided for CoShelf Square account
  • Maker is responsible in providing to the Retailer with necessary knowledge and materials in regards to the product, usage, brand, customer service for the purpose of facilitating the sale and marketing of the product on Maker’s behalf.
  • Maker is highly encouraged to promote their displayed products in the rented space through their website, social networks and word of mouth.
  • Maker is welcome to add the Retailer as a stockist on Maker’s website.
  • Maker agrees and allows all logos and images provided or taken at the Retail location to be used for marketing and promotional purposes by both CoShelf and the Retailer.
RISK AND INSURANCE
  • Maker shall have own product liability insurance for any products and materials on display at the Retail location.
  • The Retailer is responsible for any loss or damage to the products due to Retailer negligence including related personnel, staff, partners or suppliers.
  • CoShelf is responsible for any loss or damage to the products due to CoShelf negligence including related personnel, staff, partners or suppliers.
  • In the case of loss, theft or damage, an inspection will be conducted at full discretion by CoShelf in conjunction with the Retailer and the local authorities.
  • In the case where loss, theft or damage is determined to be at fault of shoppers, Maker shall be compensated for the cost price of the product, defined as 50% of the listed retail price provided to CoShelf
PRODUCT RETAIL PRICE
  • The retail price of products is entirely set by the Maker.
  • A pricelist before tax will be submitted to CoShelf with the booking confirmation.
  • CoShelf Square payment system will charge PST & GST on each product sold. CoShelf will only hold this amount on behalf the Maker and the full amount including tax will be remitted to the maker at time of payout.
  • Maker shall be responsible for the payment of all GST taxes incurred from sales of product.
  • No "sale" or "discount" is permitted in rented space.
RENTAL PERIOD:
  • The minimum Rental period is one month interval with the possibility to book several months at a time.
  • This period can be extended by booking for the following month(s) and if shelf space is available at time of booking.
TIME OF PAYMENT:
  • Maker shall pay for the first month of rental at the time of booking. For this effect, Maker will receive a PayPal link. and shall finalize payment before upshelving.
  • For booking extending over the period of one month, maker shall pay the rental fees on a month-to-month basis during the rental period. First payment will be made at booking with subsequent payments on the day before the start of a new month.
  • Maker hereby agrees to automatic withdrawal through PayPal from the time of booking.
SALES REPORT AND PAYOUT:
  • Maker payout for products sold shall be sent by CoShelf bi-weekly along with a report of the total sales & inventory.
  • CoShelf shall remit to Maker 100% revenue from the sales as reported excluding any bank or transaction fees occurred during purchase of the product or remittance.
INSPECTION AND RESTOCKING:
  • The Retailer agrees to permit the Maker to enter the premises upon agreed times to examine, inspect, and restock products which have been sold. Maker shall not remain at the retail point-of-sale to sell products themselves.
  • The Retailer or any of its representatives shall once every 2 weeks take a physical inventory of the products against stock level report and verify if any products have been damaged or removed from the premises.
NEW OR EXCHANGE OF PRODUCTS ON DISPLAY
  • The Maker shall update CoShelf on new inventory or exchange of products to be made on shelf.
  • The Maker shall book an appointment to upshelf with CoShelf and ensure proper communication at check-in to ensure that CoShelf’s system is in sync with current/new inventory.
TERMINATION:
  • Maker may terminate with prior notice and receive payout for any products sold. However, Maker shall lose the rental fee up to the end of the current rental month. Termination will cancel automatic withdrawal for subsequent months. Any unsold products shall be retrieved at a time agreed or the expense of delivery shall be paid by the Maker.
  • Retailer may terminate with prior notice and shall return to Maker all of the unsold property and bear all expenses of the delivery of the products back to Maker where applicable. CoShelf shall remit to Maker immediately following notice final payout for any products sold together along with sales report and partial refund of remaining length of rental paid.
  • In any circumstance that the space is cancelled, CoShelf will not provide any compensation for loss of income.
